DAM-OG Self Help Organization

About Us

DAMBOJ Self Help Organization is a non-profit donation company based in Ethiopia. With a strong commitment to making a positive impact on the lives of underprivileged communities, we strive to alleviate poverty, promote education, and improve overall well-being.

Mission

Our mission is to extend a helping hand to those in need, working towards reducing poverty and creating equal opportunities for all. We believe in the power of collective effort and aim to empower individuals and communities through our various initiatives.

What We Do

Education: We firmly believe in the transformative power of education. Through our initiatives, we provide educational resources, scholarships, and infrastructure support to students and schools in disadvantaged areas. By bridging the educational gap, we create a pathway to success and a brighter future.

Basic Necessities

We understand the importance of access to basic necessities for a dignified life. DAMBOJ is dedicated to providing essential supplies, such as food, clothing, and medical assistance, to individuals and families facing economic hardships. Our goal is to improve their quality of life and meet their immediate needs.

Community Development

We actively engage with local communities to identify their specific needs and design tailored solutions. We organize and facilitate community development initiatives, focusing on sustainable projects that empower communities and promote self-sufficiency.

Transparency and Accountability

At DAMBOJ, we value transparency and accountability. We ensure that all donations are used efficiently and effectively, with regular reporting and updates provided to our donors and stakeholders. We believe in building trust and maintaining long-term relationships with our supporters. Join us in our mission to create a more equitable and prosperous Ethiopia. Together, we can bring about sustainable change and empower communities for a brighter future.

Get Involved

We welcome volunteers, partners, and donors who share our vision and want to make a difference. Your support can help us create a more equitable and prosperous Ethiopia. Join us in our mission to empower communities and bring about sustainable change.
